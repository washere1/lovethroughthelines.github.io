Who We Are

Creating connection with simple acts of care

Love Through the Lines is a student-led organization dedicated to bridging the educational gap for underserved youth by providing books, school supplies, and meaningful learning activities.

With 12 active chapters across California, we empower students from low-income and marginalized backgrounds through advocacy, action, and community care. Together, we believe that education is a powerful tool to create brighter futures and stronger communities.

Each year, we organize:

  • Book donations and drives
  • Backpack kits
  • Inspiring bookmarks

Active Chapters

At Love Through the Lines, every chapter, every kit, and every book is a step toward bridging the educational gap for underserved youth. Through grassroots efforts led by passionate high school students, we’ve made a meaningful difference. But we’re not done yet. With every new volunteer and every donation, we get closer to a future where every student has the tools to thrive.
